Outreach | TBD | Director of Outreach

Welcome to Outreach! The goal of Outreach is to spread the knowledge of the STEM field to the youth of Sacramento. I oversee Thriving Youth Program and Noche de Ciencas. Within the Thriving Youth program, we hold the monthly STEM Nights. During the STEM Night’s we do mini science projects with the after-school program there. Noche de Ciencas is similar to the STEM Nights but it is an event held in an elementary school and bigger than the STEM Nights. Both events are focused to inform the Sacramento youth the STEM is not a scary field to be involved in. What my committee does is they help me plan these two events. We come up with the projects to Noche de Ciencas and help organize the day. You are always welcome to pitch ideas and events pertaining to STEM that we can be involved in. I also will try to inform you on any community service event that is around Sac State involving STEM, so that you can get those community service hours done as soon as possible. If you like getting involved in the community and helping the youth in spreading the cheer of STEM come join my Outreach Committee.

Community Service | Via Email | President

In The Servant as Leader, Robert K. Greenleaf said:

“The servant-leader is servant first… It begins with the natural feeling that one wants to serve, to serve first. Then conscious choice brings one to aspire to lead."

"A servant-leader focuses primarily on the growth and well-being of people and the communities to which they belong. While traditional leadership generally involves the accumulation and exercise of power by one at the “top of the pyramid,” servant leadership is different. The servant-leader shares power, puts the needs of others first and helps people develop and perform as highly as possible."

We will try to schedule one Community Servant event every month. The community Servant Events include but not limited to feeding the homeless, can food drive, Toy Drive, and Clothing Drive. If you would like to become a Servant Leader, you can start by putting the needs of others first and help perform these acts of kindness and generosity within the community.


This Committee will have no per-schedule meeting dates, all information and planning will be via email or phone messages. Community service hours will be given for MEP and two skill set will be awarded (Community Service and Leadership).
